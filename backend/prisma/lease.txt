4th step 

    - People on the Lease
        What's the difference between tenants and additional occupants?
            1. Tenants live at the property, sign the lease, and are financially responsible—typically anyone 18 years of age or older.
            2. Additional occupants live at the property, either full-time or part-time, but do not sign the lease—typically dependents like children or elderly relatives. For room rentals, do not include occupants that will not be staying in the room.
